You're a fit if

The approach

Not another AI course. A ship-first cohort for operators already in motion.

01

Tools, not toys.

Week one you ship something an agency client would actually pay for. A lead qualifier, a pricing calculator, a branded report, a client portal. Real deliverables, not to-do apps.

02

Two modes.

You learn to ship at two speeds. Fast — client deliverables that go from brief Monday to invoice Friday. Deep — custom apps that justify higher rates and harder-to-replace positioning. The skill is knowing which to reach for, when.

03

Access is the product.

Ship something real and you get amplified on my platforms, introduced into the right rooms, and invited into the ecosystem 17 years of Austin operators trust. The software is the admission fee. The room is the reward.
