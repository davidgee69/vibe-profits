Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

We care about your privacy and we try to keep this short. Here's what we collect, why we collect it, and what you can do about it.

What we collect

  • Email address. When you fill out our form to join the list.
  • Basic analytics. Pages visited, rough location, device type. We use this to understand what's working.
  • Payment info. If you buy something, payment is handled by Stripe. We don't store your card number.

How we use it

  • Send you the emails you signed up for
  • Let you know when cohorts and products open
  • Improve the site and the offer
  • Fulfill any purchases you make

Who we share it with

We use a few trusted services to run the business. They only see what they need to do their job:

  • Email provider — to send emails
  • Stripe — to process payments
  • Analytics provider — to understand site traffic

We don't sell your data. Ever.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tech for basic site functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser if you prefer; some parts of the site may not work as well.

Your rights

You can:

  • Unsubscribe from emails any time (link at the bottom of each email)
  • Ask us what we have on you
  • Ask us to delete what we have on you
  • Ask us to correct something

To do any of that, email privacy@vibeprofits.com and we'll handle it within a reasonable time.

Children

Vibe Profits is not for children under 16. If you're under 16, please don't submit your email.

Data retention

We keep your email and account data as long as you're on the list or an active customer. If you unsubscribe and haven't bought anything, we'll remove your record from active use.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. If the changes are significant, we'll email you.

Contact

Questions? Email privacy@vibeprofits.com.
